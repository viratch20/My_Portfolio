CRM Data Analysis

Project Overview

     In this project, I conducted a comprehensive analysis of customer data to identify trends, insights, and opportunities for optimization. This is a detailed operational dashboard showing the day-to-day operations of a customer support department. It allows the team and stakeholders to monitor the status of ongoing activities and make adjustments based on data.

Objectives

    Analyze historical customer cases data to identify key trends and patterns. Evaluate the performance of different status, states, regions, and service centers. Develop actionable insights and recommendations to drive customer care growth.The primary objective was to enhance customer cases performance and inform strategic decision-making for the business.

Key Findings

   Key Findings Identified frequent issues faced by customers, allowing for targeted improvements in product/service offerings. Analyzed response times and found correlations between speed of service and customer satisfaction. Highlighted training needs for customer service representatives based on performance.
